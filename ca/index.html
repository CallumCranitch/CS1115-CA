A war fought on the land
A war fought in the hills

A war fought in the seas
The second world war was a brutal conflict that went on for 6 years, in that time unimaginable human suffering occured, approximatly 34246 thousand people died every day for the duration of the conflict which is 1426 people an hour 23 people a minute and one person every 3 seconds for 6 years straight words cannot describe how massive that is.

This website aims to spread information about the events that occured the innovations that came from the conflict and try and clear the air around any Misconceptions
